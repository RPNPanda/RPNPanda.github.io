Cold War Zombie Tool

MUST BE HOST IN ORDER TO WORK!

  1. Open Loader.exe and enter your key
  2. Start Cold War and enter a zombies lobby
  3. Click "Click In Zombie Lobby" in the loader logs
  4. Wait for prompt: "Load into a Zombie game and kill 1 zombie"
  5. Kill one zombie
  6. Open the menu and click "Attach"
  7. Wait for: Addresses Catch (1/6)...(6/6)
  8. Enjoy! Enable cheats after all addresses are caught

If stuck at 5/6: tool is outdated or you didn’t kill a zombie
